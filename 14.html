Fashion Battle

Game Instructions

Game Introduction

Fashion Battle is an exciting fashion competition game where you compete against other players in creating stunning outfits. Show your fashion sense and creativity to become the ultimate style champion!

Game Rules

  • Choose outfits that match the given theme
  • Mix and match different clothing items strategically
  • Complete your look within the time limit
  • Earn points based on style rating and audience votes

Controls

  • Click or tap to select clothing items
  • Drag and drop to arrange accessories
  • Use color palette to customize your design
  • Submit button to complete your look

Game Features

  • AI-powered style rating system
  • Real-time fashion competitions
  • Seasonal collections and trending styles
  • Global fashion leaderboards
  • Daily style challenges
  • Virtual runway shows
  • Fashion design workshops
  • Style community features
