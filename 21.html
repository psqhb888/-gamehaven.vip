Game Introduction

Peet Sneak is a clever stealth puzzle game where you guide Peet through shadowy corridors and challenging environments. Collect keys, outsmart guards, and solve puzzles to reach your destination. Use your wits and timing to master the art of sneaking!

Game Rules

  • Navigate through shadowy corridors
  • Collect keys to unlock doors
  • Avoid detection by guards
  • Solve environmental puzzles
  • Find the safest path to your goal

Controls

  • Click and hold to move Peet
  • Release to stop movement
  • Time your movements carefully
  • Use shadows to hide from guards
  • Plan your route strategically

Game Features

  • Stealth-based gameplay
  • Environmental puzzles
  • Guard patrol patterns
  • Key collection system
  • Multiple solution paths
  • Progressive difficulty
  • Shadow mechanics
  • Strategic planning elements
