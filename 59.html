Neon Swing Game

Game Instructions

Game Introduction

Swing as fast as you can in Neon Swing! Swing from point to point and try to get as far as you can while using trampolins to get even further. Try to avoid the red wall, which is slowly moving towards you, since once in the wall, you lose! The trampolins in your way can also make your life a bit more difficult, so use them to your advantage. Make clever use of the game mechanics and be the best neon swing player out there!

Game Rules

  • Swing between points
  • Use trampolines for extra distance
  • Avoid the red wall
  • Get as far as possible
  • Plan your swings carefully

Controls

  • Click to swing
  • Time your swings
  • Use trampolines strategically
  • Watch for the red wall
  • React quickly to obstacles

Game Features

  • Endless gameplay
  • Trampoline mechanics
  • Moving red wall
  • Increasing difficulty
  • Quick reflexes required
  • Simple controls
  • Addictive gameplay
  • Fun challenges
