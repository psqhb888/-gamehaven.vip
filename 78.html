Game Instructions

Game Introduction

Mix a relaxing ASMR experience with a stunning 3D environment and you get the perfect game for stressful times - Pop It! 3D The gameplay is rather simple and of course relaxing. Select a great looking Pop It and pop all the bubbles on one side to turn around the model and continue on the other side. While playing there is a chance of finding coins in popped bubbles, which you can spend to unlock new Pop Its! Feeling creative? Play around with different colors and motives and create your own perfect Pop It! Prepare for a relaxing time and say goodbye to all your stress!

Game Rules

  • Pop bubbles on one side
  • Turn the model to continue
  • Collect coins from bubbles
  • Unlock new Pop It designs
  • Create custom color combinations
  • Enjoy relaxing gameplay
  • Find hidden surprises
  • Complete all sides

Controls

  • Click to pop bubbles
  • Rotate the 3D model
  • Select different Pop It designs
  • Customize colors and patterns
  • Collect coins
  • Unlock new designs
